About

Hi, I’m Joice — the person behind Kogame.

I started Kogame Popculture because I love finding gaming items that still have life left in them. Some pieces go to collectors, some go to people rebuilding childhood memories, and some are just too cool to leave behind.

The idea is simple: find good items, describe them properly, and help them reach someone who will actually enjoy them.
